Our Speaker - MD

Mr. Akash Anand

A first-generation entrepreneur, Akash Anand started his journey with a dream of making daily household available to everyone and serving the Indian nation. With this vision he launched his own Brands, Retail Networks, Finserv, Infotech & Insurance and invest heavily in creating the country’s leading consumption ecosystem. He embodies the organization’s credo, ‘Innovation beyond Imagination’ and considers Indianness as the core value driving the organization.

Akash earned a Graduation Degree in Business Management from Plymouth University, England. He also earned Diploma in finance from City university London. Akash provides overall leadership to Djt Group and spends his time in strengthening the Parent Brand amongst entrepreneurs, company managements, financiers and Stakeholders.
