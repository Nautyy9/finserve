ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA FOR SECURE BUSINESS LOAN

The below criteria varies according to the requirements of the borrower. So, it is advisable to check the eligibility criteria with your lender before proceeding for the loan.

Should be a self-employed individual, proprietor, Private Ltd. Co. or a Partnership Firm that is involved in the business of manufacturing, trading or services.

Should have a minimum of 3 to 4 years of business experience in the current business.

The applicant should be at least 21 years at the time of applying for the loan, and should be no older than 65 years at the time of loan maturity.

HOW TO APPLY FOR A BUSINESS LOAN?

The simple process to apply for a business loan is as follow:

You can raise the query for a business loan through online or offline channels. You can either apply directly through our official website by clicking on www.djtfintech.com or you may contact our customer executive on phone.

Once the query is being raised, our executive will visit your office. The customer needs to fill the mandatory details of the form. Once the document verification will be done, we will login and check your CIBIL score on the basis of which loan will be approved.

Credit manager within 2 days, will visit the shop and verify the business details and property, stock and sales of the business etc. The physical personal meeting of borrower and credit manager will help the customer to understand all the terms and conditions, procedures, EMIs and loan applicability in a better way.

The official Credit Manager and borrower will visit the property for invigilation.

The final loan amount by the executive will be discussed after the survey of the property and the customer if satisfied, needs to sign on the agreement of loan amount and property papers.

Your application will be approved, and the loan amount will be disbursed into your bank account.
